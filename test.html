This level of detail is used to show basic envelope dimensions of our equipment and simplified representations of existing structure and equipment. The drawings for this level of detail will contain the following views, with indicated dimensions and notes:
  • Plan View
    • Channel width at seal area
    • Channel recess width (if applicable)
    • Flow direction icon
    • View indicators for Front and Side views
  • Front View (Looking in Flow Direction)
    • Channel width
    • Overall screen height
    • Screen height from top of channel (shown as ref)
    • Channel height
  • Side View
    • D1 and D2 icons
    • Channel height

The following must also be included, but may be shown in different views depending on the equipment proposed:
  • Compactor discharge height (if applicable)
  • Overall length of proposed equipment
  • Sluice height (if applicable)
  • Note indicating size and type of screen openings (ie "6mm Perforated Openings")
  • Notes indicating model(s) of proposed equipment

The concrete and existing structure included in drawings of this level of detail will be shown more accurately than in Level 1 drawings, but it will not be dimensioned. Basic representations of existing or newly proposed equipment will be shown as reference and labelled but will not be dimensioned or dimensioned to.
